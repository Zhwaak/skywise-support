## Full Privacy Policy
PRIVACY POLICY
1. Information We Collect
Skywise Part 107 Pro collects the following information:
Study Progress: We store your answer attempts, study sessions, and performance data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
Subscription Status: We use Apple's StoreKit to verify your subscription status. This information is processed through Apple's secure servers.
Device Information: Basic device information may be collected for app functionality and crash reporting through Apple's standard analytics.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
Provide personalized study recommendations and track your progress
Improve the App's features and user experience
Process subscription payments through Apple's payment system
Provide customer support when requested
3. Data Storage and Security
Your study data and progress are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This means your personal study information never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it. We do not transmit your study data to external servers. Subscription information is securely processed through Apple's StoreKit and iCloud, following Apple's privacy standards.
4. Third-Party Services
The App uses the following third-party services:
Apple App Store: For app distribution and subscription management
StoreKit: For in-app purchases and subscription verification (handled by Apple)
iCloud: Optional syncing of your data across devices (controlled by your iCloud settings)
All third-party services are provided by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
5. Smart Wingman Feature
The Smart Wingman feature provides explanations using locally-generated responses based on question content. Your questions and answers are not sent to external AI services or third-party servers. All processing occurs on your device, and no personal information is transmitted.
6. Children's Privacy
Skywise Part 107 Pro is intended for users who are at least 13 years old, as required for FAA Part 107 certification. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
7. Your Rights
You have the right to:
Access your data stored locally on your device
Delete your local data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data through iOS settings
Control your subscription through Apple's subscription management in Settings
Opt out of analytics through your device settings
8. Data Retention
Your study data is retained on your device for as long as you have the App installed. If you uninstall the App, all local data is deleted. Subscription information is retained by Apple according to their policies.
9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes. Your continued use of the App after such modifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
10. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store listing or visit skywise.app.
